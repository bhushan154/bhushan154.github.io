I, Bhushan Nagaraj, am a JIRA developer with over 3 years of experience administering and customizing Atlassian JIRA.

During my initial weeks as a JIRA developer, I tried to gather as many examples as possible from the Atlassian developer site. I gained a lot of interest in Atlassian JIRA administration and development. I started documenting tutorials of all the different plugin modules available.

I am now sharing all these tutorials through www.jiradev.com as an attempt to create a structured learning path for developers who are new to JIRA plugin development.

If you found this site helpful, please show your support by tweeting about it.
